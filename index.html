Hi,

I'm Pragathi Ilango, a UI/UX designer and web developer. This is my AI explorations space — a place where I document projects I build while experimenting with prompting, new tools, and interaction ideas.

I add everything here, no matter how small, strange, or unfinished. Some projects start as quick prompting experiments, some explore interaction concepts, and others are ideas that once drove me slightly insane.

Each project is a learning snapshot — what I tried, what broke, what I learned, and what I improved.

AI Works

Photo Wall

Photo Wall is a small interactive web experiment where users can upload images and arrange them on a dynamic, polaroid-style board.

Built using: Base44 · ChatGPT

Shared Table (Contra x Figma Makeathon)

A minimalist digital recipe archive inspired by handwritten recipe cards. Recipes can be saved, organized into simple tabs, and downloaded as keepsake-style cards.

Built using: Figma Make · ChatGPT

Pet Store Template (BunBox Burrow)

A playful boutique-style pet store template designed with warm visuals and friendly layouts.

Built using: Replit

DinnerMate AI (Gemini 3 Hackathon)

Your kitchen companion that adapts recipes based on your time, skill level, and confidence, turning them into clear step-by-step cooking plans so you can enjoy your favorite dishes.

Built using: Gemini

Figma Portfolios

An exploration of portfolio design using Figma Make, experimenting with prompts to generate a range of layouts from creative concepts to clean, minimal interfaces.

Built using: Figma Make · ChatGPT

The Mood Lot

A kawaii-style sticker generator that creates fun faces to capture your daily mood.

Built using: Figma Make · ChatGPT
