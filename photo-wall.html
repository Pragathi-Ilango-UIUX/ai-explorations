Photo Wall

Photo Wall is a small interactive web experiment where users can upload images and arrange them on a dynamic, polaroid-style board. I wanted to explore a playful “drag and rearrange” interaction with instant visual feedback.

I built this as a lightweight practice project: starting from a simple idea, using AI to move quickly, and keeping the result minimal and fun.

Built using: Base44 · ChatGPT
